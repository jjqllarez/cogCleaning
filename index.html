About Us

Who We Are

Get to know the team behind the sparkle

Your Trusted Family-Owned Cleaning Partner

Founded with a mission to deliver exceptional cleaning services, COG Cleaning LLC is a family-owned and operated business that has grown into one of the most trusted names in residential and commercial cleaning throughout Virginia. With over 7 years of hands-on experience in the cleaning industry, we have built our reputation on reliability, thoroughness, and the genuine care that only a family-run company can provide for our clients' spaces.

We believe that a clean environment is the foundation of a healthy, productive life. That is why our trained and insured teams use eco-friendly products and proven techniques to ensure every surface shines — from kitchens and bathrooms to offices and common areas. As a family business, your satisfaction is not just our goal; it is our personal guarantee.

Whether you need a one-time deep clean, regular maintenance, or a comprehensive move-out service, we tailor every visit to your specific needs. No corner is overlooked, no detail is too small, and with us, no client is ever just a number.
